# Coffee Orders Sales Performance Dashboard

**Tools:** Excel · Pivot Tables · Dashboard · Timeline Slicers

## About
Analyzed 4 years (2019–2022) of coffee order data across 3 countries 
(US, Ireland, UK) and 4 coffee types (Arabica, Excelsa, Liberica, Robusta) 
to identify sales trends, top customers, and regional performance.

## Key Insight
The US contributed 78% of total revenue ($35.6K out of $45.1K). 
Liberica recorded its highest single-month sales in Jan 2022 ($843), 
signalling a strong regional demand spike.

## Dataset
Source: Kaggle — Coffee Orders Dataset

## Skills Demonstrated
- Data Cleaning & Structuring
- Pivot Tables (monthly/yearly breakdown by coffee type)
- Country-wise Revenue Bar Chart
- Top 5 Customers Leaderboard
- Interactive Dashboard with Timeline Slicers & Loyalty Card Filters
